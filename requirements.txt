CLOTHING STORE ACCOUNTING & MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

==============================
OVERVIEW
==============================

This project is a Clothing Store Management System integrated with basic accounting features.

The system helps manage:
- Sales (Invoices)
- Inventory (Stock & Cost)
- Expenses
- Supplier payments
- Staff activities
- Financial reports

==============================
OBJECTIVES
==============================

- Manage product inventory efficiently
- Track revenue and expenses
- Calculate profit and loss
- Support staff operations
- Provide financial insights through reports

==============================
DATA MODELS
==============================

1. Product
- id: product identifier
- name: product name
- category: product category
- size: (S, M, L, XL)
- color: product color
- material: product material
- price: selling price
- costPrice: cost price
- stock: available quantity
- minThreshold: minimum stock alert level

------------------------------

2. Customer
- id: customer identifier
- name: customer name
- phone: phone number
- totalSpent: total spending
- rank: (NORMAL, SILVER, GOLD, VIP)

------------------------------

3. Staff
- id: staff identifier
- name: staff name
- phone: phone number
- role: (ADMIN, MANAGER, STAFF)
- salary: salary amount

------------------------------

4. Account
- id: account identifier
- staffId: linked staff ID
- username: login username
- password: login password
- role: (ADMIN, MANAGER, STAFF)

------------------------------

5. Invoice
- id: invoice identifier
- customerId: customer ID (optional)
- staffId: staff who created the invoice
- items: list of purchased items
- totalAmount: total amount
- discount: discount value
- finalAmount: final payable amount
- paymentMethod: (CASH, BANK_TRANSFER, CARD, E_WALLET)
- createdAt: creation date

------------------------------

6. InvoiceItem
- productId: product ID
- quantity: quantity sold
- price: selling price
- cost: cost price

------------------------------

7. Purchase
- id: purchase identifier
- supplier: supplier name
- staffId: staff responsible
- items: list of purchased items
- totalCost: total cost
- createdAt: creation date

------------------------------

8. PurchaseItem
- productId: product ID
- quantity: quantity purchased
- costPrice: purchase cost

------------------------------

9. InventoryBatch
- id: batch identifier
- productId: product ID
- quantity: quantity in batch
- costPrice: batch cost price
- importedAt: import date
- createdBy: creator (staffId)

------------------------------

10. Transaction
- id: transaction identifier
- type: (INCOME)
- category: (SALES, PURCHASE, SALARY, RENT, MARKETING, REFUND)
- amount: transaction amount
- paymentMethod: (CASH, BANK_TRANSFER, CARD, E_WALLET)
- referenceId: related entity ID (invoice/purchase)
- createdBy: staffId
- createdAt: creation date

------------------------------

11. Expenses
- id: expense identifier
- type: (FIXED, VARIABLE)
- amount: expense amount
- description: expense description
- paymentMethod: (CASH, BANK_TRANSFER, CARD, E_WALLET)
- createdBy: staffId
- date: expense date

------------------------------

12. Refund
- id: refund identifier
- invoiceId: related invoice ID
- items: returned items
- refundAmount: total refund amount
- createdBy: staffId
- createdAt: creation date

------------------------------

13. ReturnItem
- productId: product ID
- quantity: returned quantity

------------------------------

14. PeriodReport
- revenue: total revenue
- cogs: cost of goods sold
- expenses: total expenses
- grossProfit: gross profit
- netProfit: net profit
- momGrowth: month-over-month growth
- yoyGrowth: year-over-year growth
- totalOrders: total number of orders
- aov: average order value

==============================
FEATURES
==============================

1. Sales Management
- Create invoices
- Calculate totals and discounts
- Immediate payment processing
- Automatically generate transactions
- Deduct inventory stock

------------------------------

2. Inventory Management
- Track stock quantity
- Import goods
- Manage inventory batches for cost calculation
- Low stock alerts

------------------------------

3. Accounting
- Record income and expenses
- Track supplier payments/debts
- Monitor cash flow

------------------------------

4. Refund Management
- Handle partial or full returns
- Restore inventory
- Record refund transactions

------------------------------

5. Staff Management
- Manage employees
- Role-based access control:
  ADMIN | MANAGER | STAFF

------------------------------

6. Reporting
- Profit & Loss (P&L)
- Revenue tracking
- Expense tracking
- Sales performance analysis
